## Components

### 1. Infection Classification
- **Purpose:** Identifies one of 7 potato disease classes (including Healthy).
- **Location:** `Infection Classification/`
- **Architecture:** Transitioning from EfficientNet-B0 to MobileNet-V2.
- **Key Files:** `Infection Classification/train.py`, `Infection Classification/GOAL.md`.
- **Special Logic:** Uses custom `RandomGridOrCrop` to simulate 4x4 grid deployment.

### 2. Leaf Segmentation
- **Purpose:** Segments individual leaves in the frame.
- **Location:** `Leaf Segmentation/`
- **Architecture:** YOLOv8n-seg (Optimized for 640x640).
- **Key Files:** `Leaf Segmentation/train.py`, `Leaf Segmentation/GOAL.md`.

### 3. Lesion Segmentation
- **Purpose:** Identifies and segments individual lesion spots on leaves.
- **Location:** `Lesion Segmentation/`
- **Architecture:** UNet with MobileNet-V2 encoder (Optimized for 256x256).
- **Key Files:** `Lesion Segmentation/train.py`, `Lesion Segmentation/GOAL.md`.

## Data Flow

1. **Preprocessing:** Images are captured and resized/padded to match model requirements (224/640/256).
2. **Inference:** Models run on Hailo-8L NPU.
3. **Post-processing:** Masks and classification labels are synthesized for the user.
